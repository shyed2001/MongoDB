# Obsidian Linking Policy

## Link Style

Use wiki links for human-readable vault navigation:

- [[000_MASTER_INDEX]]
- [[002_REPO_REGISTRY_INDEX]]
- [[crossrefs/all-repo-crossrefs]]

Use plain paths for machine-readable scripts.

## Rule

Every major doc should link upward to the master index and sideways to related repo/tool/MCP/server docs.
